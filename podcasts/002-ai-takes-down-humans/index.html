002: AI takes down the humans

The tech year’s getting into full swing again, so we’ve got a bunch of great things to discuss this week! Here’s what we talk about on Charged 002:

  • Google’s AI beating humans 4-1 at the infinitely complex game of Go
    • Here’s the link to the book Superintelligence by Nick Bostrom mentioned by John

  • Opera adding ad-blocking software natively into its browser
    • Blendle, the service that lets you pay for news without any hassle
